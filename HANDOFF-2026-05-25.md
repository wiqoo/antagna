## Strict design rules to honor (D-034)

If you're about to write any UI code, check these against your output:

- ✅ Single accent: orange `#FF6B1A` only.
- ✅ Backgrounds: `#0F0F12` / `#17171C`.
- ✅ Hairline borders: `rgba(255,255,255,0.06–0.16)`.
- ❌ **No** chromatic gradients (purple/cyan/pink/emerald used as decoration).
- ❌ **No** mesh gradients, glow shadows, frosted glass decoration.
- ❌ **No** project codes as primary identifiers (names lead, codes
  go to subtitle/tooltip).
- ✅ Premium feel comes from typography + spacing + rhythm — not color.

Full context in `memory/feedback_design_taste.md`. Read it before
proposing anything new.
